Meet The Team

VRHome wouldnt exist if it wasn't for our hardworking development team, they work 24/7 to ensure that new updates are pushed, device compatibility is increased, and stability is ensured.


Martin D'Abdore

Martin D'Abdore is the founder of VRHome. He envisioned the product as a way to blur the lines between physical and virtual realities by allowing them to be controlled by one another to create a thoroughly immersive experience.

Thomas Righton

Thomas Righton joined VRHome in late 2024, his boundless knowledge on smarthome systems being a guiding light through tough developmental cycles.

Michelle Jackson

Michelle Jackson, the self-made millionaire, acquired VRHome for $3.9m in early 2022. Her continued trust and investment in the ideas of VRHome has led to our projects continued growth and development.
