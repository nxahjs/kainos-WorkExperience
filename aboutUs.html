What Is VRHome?

VRHome is a virtual reality program created to control your smart home from a Virtual Reality Headset


Why?

We knew it must be easier than it is now to combine the virtual world with the physical. A blending of the physical and virtual realities through the home.


How is it going to work?

VRHome is going to be connected with all smart devices in a home with a single internet connection and will be manipulatable by using hand signals like waving at a lightbulb to turn it on


When?

We are aiming to release VRHome in the next few months
